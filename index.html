How to Play

Play Maze Madness:

  • Move your square through the maze to the chequered flag using the arrow keys.

  • You start with 1000 points. For every move you make you lose 10 points. For every level you complete you gain 5000 points.

  • Be aware of the timer! If this runs out, it's game over. You start with 10 seconds. Any time you have left over from the previous level is carried forward to the next level.

  • Look out for gold coins! If you collect one of these, you gain 10 extra seconds.

Maze in Your Own Time:

  • No timer, no score, no pressure! Just enjoy mazing
  • (Look out for the "Show Route" button for a helping hint!)
